The Valley of Geysers

What to do in the Valley of Geysers?

The Valley of Geysers is a beautiful area to view, but it is quite dangerous. It is highly recommended that you travel to the Valley of Geysers with a guide due to the danger the area poses without guidance.

Where is the Valley of Geysers?

The Valley of Geysers is aprt of the Kronotsky Nature Reserve in Russia. The Valley of Geysers is a canyon stretching 8 kilometers long, 4 kilometers wide and is approximately 400 meters deep. If you would like to see a more detailed map of the Valley of Geysers, click the image to the right.

How to get to the Valley of Geysers?

The Valley of Geysers is very difficult to reach so helicopters are the only safe way of transport. If you want to learn more about your options of travel and tour guides, click the image to the left.
