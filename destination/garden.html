Hakuryu-en Garden

What to do in Hakuryu-en Garden?

The Hakuryu-en Garden is only open to visitors during during the autumn leaves and cherry blossoms seasons in Kyoto. During these seasons, it is still tough to see the garden because they only accept 100 people per day. In order to increase your chance at acquiring a ticket, its advised that you go early in the morning. If you would like to learn more about Hakuryu-en Garden, click the image to the left.

Where is Hakuryu-en Garden?

Hakuryu-en Garden is located in the southern central part of Japan within Kyoto. If you would like to see a more detailed map of the Hakuryu-en Garden area and Kyoto, click the image to the right.

How to get to Hakuryu-en Garden?

The closest airport to Kyoto is Kansai International Airport in Osaka. From Osaka you will travel to Eiden's Demachiyanagi Station in Kyoto which is where you buy the tickets to get into Hakuryu-en Garden.
