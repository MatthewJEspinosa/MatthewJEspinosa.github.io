Cap Sainte-Marie

What to do at Cap Sainte-Marie?

There is not too many things to do in the Cap Sainte-Marie Reserve, but some of the most popular things that you can do include, whale watching during humpback migration season, short hike trails, visit the lighthouse and exploring the beachside caves below the cliffs.
