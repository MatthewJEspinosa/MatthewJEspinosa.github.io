Southwest National Park

What to do in Southwest National Park?

While visiting Southwest National Park there are many activites for you to take part in. You can go fishing, kayaking, scuba diving, snorkelling, caving and if you decide to camp on the premises, you can even barbeque. There are also plenty of short hikes and even a multi-day hike called the South Coast Track. Where is Southwest National Park?

Southwest National Park is located on the southwestern tip of Tasmania, Austrailia (which is why it is called Southwest National Park). If you would like to see a more detailed map of Southwest National Park, click the image to the right.

How to get to Southwest National Park?

There are multiple ways for you to get to Southwest National Park. One way is by driving on the A6 from Hobart to Cockle Creek. Another way is by taking the B61 from Mount Field to reach Lake Gordon.
