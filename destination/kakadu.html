Kakadu National Park

What to do in Kakadu National Park?

A visit to Kakadu National Park is best for people who enjoy activites that are sometimes physically demanding and require patience. While at Kakadu National Park, you can bird watch, search for crocodiles, fish, and embark on guided or independent hikes to see the waterfalls. Where is Kakadu National Park?

Kakadu National Park is located on the northern tip in the Northern Territory of Austrailia. Something particularly interesting about the area of the park is that it includes parts of forest, desert, and oceanside.
