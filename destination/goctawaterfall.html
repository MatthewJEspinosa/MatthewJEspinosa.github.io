Gocta Waterfall

What to do at the Gocta Waterfall?

When hiking the Gocta Waterfalls trail, you have the option to walk the trail or you do the trail on horseback (with additional fees). Depending on the pace of the group, the trail can last about 2+ hours. There are also places in Cocachimba where you can sleep that have a direct view of the waterfall. How to get to the Gocta Waterfall?

To start the hike to the Gocta Waterfalls, you must get to the town of Cocachimba. From there you will be able to access the Gocta Waterfalls trail. If you want to learn more about your options of travel to get to the Gocta Waterfalls, click the image to the left.
