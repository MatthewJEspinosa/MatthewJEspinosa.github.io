Misiones

What to do in Misiones?

There are quite a few things to do while visiting Misiones. You could visit Iguazu Falls, which is a collection of over 250 waterfalls that crash over a horse-shoe chaped cliff edge, adventure into the jungle, observe the incredible number of butterfly species and visit the amethyst caves. Where is Misiones?
