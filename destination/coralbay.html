Coral Bay

What to do in Coral Bay?

The main reason you may want to visit Coral Bay is to observe the exotic wildlife of the western Austrailian coast. This includes Coral Bay Beach where you could go swimming, Ningaloo Marine Park where you could see the magnificent Whale Shark, along with plenty of other wildlife, and you can even dive at the Ningaloo Reef. Where is Coral Bay?

Coral Bay is a small coastal town in the Gascoyne region of Western Austrailia. It is located approximately 1,200 kilometers north of Perth. If you would like to see a more detailed map of Coral Bay, click the image to the right.

How to get to Coral Bay?

To get to Coral Bay, you could drive northward from Perth along the Coral Coast Highway which would take approximately 12 hours. You could also fly to Exmouth and then drive southward to Coral Bay which the drive would take approximately 2 hours.
