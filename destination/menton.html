Menton

What to do in Menton?

There are a variety of things that you can do while in Menton. There are a few gardens that you can visit including Val Rahmeh Botanical Garden, there are museums including the Jean Cocteau Museum, there are beaches where you can relax and you can also explore the town, marvelling in its beauty while trying the local cuisine.
