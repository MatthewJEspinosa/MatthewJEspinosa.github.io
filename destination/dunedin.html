What to do in Dunedin?

There are plenty of things to do and sights to see while visiting Dunedin. You could visit the Otago Peninsula, the Dunedin Railway Station, the Larnach Castle and Gardens, or you could go on wildlife tours to experience the varity of wildlife in New Zealand including penguiuns!
