Visby

What to do in Visby?

While visiting Visby, some of the most interesting things you can do include visitng the Botaniska Tradgarden, DBW's Botanical Garden, the Nar Fyr lighthouse, the Lummelundagrottan caverns and the Drottens Ruins. Where is Visby?

Visby is a small town on Gotland (a Swedish island in the Baltic Sea). If you would like to see a more detailed map of Visby, click the image to the right.

How to get to Visby?

Gotland runs two ferries from Nynashamn and Oskarshamn to Visby. You can take your car onto these ferries if you desire and the ferry takes approximately 3 hours. If you want to learn more about your options of travel to Visby, click the image to the left.
