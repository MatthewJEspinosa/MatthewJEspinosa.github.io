Kafue

What to do in Kafue?

If you are interested in observing Africa's wildlife in a natural way, then Kafue National Park is a great destination. At the park you can go fishing, bird watching (considered a paradise for avid bird watchers), canoeing and there are both walking and driven safaris where you can observe the vast wildlife within the park. Where is Kafue?

Kafue is a town in the Lusaka Province of Zambia. It lies on the north bank of the Kafue River. If you would like to see a more detailed map of Kafue, click the image to the right.

How to get to Kafue?

The most common way to get to Kafue National Park is via car departing from Lusaka. There are numerous airstrips including Ngoma, Chunga and Lufupa that can be accessed via plane. If you want to learn more about your options of travel to Kafue National Park, click the image to the left.
