What to do on Cape Horn?

There are many things to do on Cape Horn including a scenic flight over the island, climbing the cliff, visiting the Lighthouse, visiting the Stella Maris Chapel and visiting the "actual" horn (this is the point where the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans meet). Another option is taking a cruise via Holland America or Celebrity Cruises to see the island and briefly stop at the island led by a guide.
