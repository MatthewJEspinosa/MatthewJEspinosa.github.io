Choquequirao

What to do in Choquequirao?

The Choquequirao trek is 64 kilometers long with a starting altitude of 2,900 meters and it peaks at 3,050 meters. The trek takes about 4 days and throughout the route you will see ancient Incan ruins along with stunning views of the Andes mountains and forest. How to get to Choquequirao?

There are three trails that lead to Choquequirao. One from Cachora, Huanipaca and Yanama. To get to Cachora or Huanipaca, you can take the bus from Cusco to Abancay and get off at Ramal. If you want to learn more about your options to get to Choquequirao, click the image to the left.
