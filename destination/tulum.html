Tulum

What to do in Tulum?

Tulum is a great destination for anyone, but especially couples. While in Tulum, if you just want to sit back and relax there are plenty of beautiful beaches to choose from and delicious local food to try. If you interested in being a little more active, you can go snorkling, scuba diving, or explore caverns and the ancient Mayan ruins. Tulum is filled with stunning housing options that are great for a romatic getaway with a significant other. Where is Tulum?

Tulum is a small town on the Caribbean coastline of Mexico's Yucatan Peninsula. To see a more detailed map of Tulum, click the image to the right.

How to get to Tulum?

The most common way to get to Tulum is by flying into Cancun International Airport and then driving to Tulum. There are some hotels/house rental companies that will arrange a private driver to pick you up from the airport.
