Bantayan Island

What to do on Bantayan Island?

There are plenty of things to do in Bantayan Island. You can sit and relax on Paradise Beach, island hop to the surronding islands, cliff dive near the island ruins and even skydive. If you would like to discover more to do while visiting Bantayan Island, click the image to the left.

Where is Bantayan Island?

Bantayan Island is a small island at the center of the Philippines. It is surrounded by Virgin Island and Hilantagaan Island which are two of the isalnds that are frequently visited by people that are island hopping. If you would like to see a more detailed map of Bantayan Island, click the image to the right.

How to get to Bantayan Island?

To get to Bantayan Island, you must fly to Cebu and then take a bus from the Northern Terminal to Hagnaya Port. From Hagnaya Port, you ride a ferry to Bantayan Island. If you want to learn more about your options of travel to and around the island, click the image to the left.
